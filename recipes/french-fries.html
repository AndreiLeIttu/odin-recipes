French Fries Recipe

Desciption

A simple recipe for classic french fries, ready in less than 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes
  • salt
  • corn oil
  • That's it!

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into long-ish box-shaped pieces after keeping the potatoes in water for a couple of minutes.
  2. Fry enough oil inside a frying pan or pot such that all the potatoes fit inside it completely and pour the sliced potatoes inside.
  3. Pour salt inside the pan and use a spatula to move the potatoes every one/two minutes.
  4. When the potatoes reach an intense yellow color, take out the potatoes and serve them while hot.
