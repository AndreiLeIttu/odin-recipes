American Omelette Recipe

Description

A quick, healthy and simple recipe for a nice breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 2/3/4 eggs(depending on number of people)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or olive oil
  • a couple parsley leaves and one green onion (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Melt the butter or pour the oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and reduce the heat to medium-low.
  4. Cook for a couple of minutes, until no liquid egg remains, and until the color matches your preference. While cooking the eggs, move a spatula across the bottom and turn the skillet to form a big whole
  5. Fold the whole in half and let the bottom cook for about 30 more seconds, then use the spatula to move the omelette to a plate.
  6. If you want, add some parsley leaves and small "rings" of green onion over the omelette for more flavor.
